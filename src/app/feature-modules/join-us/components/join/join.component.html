  • Free entry to Lincolnshire league cross country events

  • Monthly track sessions

  • Socialise together at special club events and keep running fun

Membership fee

The membership fee for 2022/2023 is remaining at £35 for the year.

This also covers affiliation with England Athletics, so you will receive your EA card soon after joining.

LDR Membership will run from 1st April 2023 to 31 March 2024. Anyone joining part way through the club year may be eligible for a reduction in the fees, depending on what time of year they join. Please bear in mind that EA affiliation costs are not pro rata so there will always be a minimum membership fee to cover this.

Club nights

The club runs start promptly at 7:00 PM, please aim to meet up a few minutes earlier for any announcements and to greet any new members.

Tuesday

Tuesday nights we start from the North Kesteven Sports Centre (LN6 9AX), except for the last Tuesday of the month, which is now our track night at LSST sports facility (LN5 8PW).

Thursday

Thursday nights we vary our start location in Lincoln and the surrounding areas, to add spice and the occasional hill to your training. Please see the training schedule for further details.

Track nights

For our track nights we charge £2 per session, payable on the night or in advance (this covers some of the clubs track hire costs, and enables us to keep the overall membership fee for the year low).
