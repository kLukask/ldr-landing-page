Club training sessions

On club nights we have a variety of groups running different activities so you can choose whatever running session you like. Our range of groups and club nights include:

  • Structured Training Session: Aimed at 5k, 10K or longer race training.
  • Athletics Track night: the last Tuesday of each month we head to LSST Athletics Track.
  • Social Groups: General running groups running between 5miles and 10K.
  • Head torch run: During Autumn / Winter.
  • Orienteering or special club events and races (i.e. club predictor run).
