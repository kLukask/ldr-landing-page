Lincoln District Runners

We are a friendly and social group of runners who meet for runs in Lincoln and the surrounding areas.

We endeavour to make our club runs fun and varied, whilst encouraging runners to develop and improve.

We also encourage members to lead run and events, and offer substantial discounts for members wishing to pursue the Leadership in Running Fitness (LIRF) qualifications.

  • Weekly structured sessions

  • Club hosted events

  • Yearly championship challenges

  • Regular trips away to the hills

  • Head-torch runs

  • Social events

Club membership

Lincoln & District Runners are keen to welcome runners to our wonderful club.

The club offers a free session with the club so you can see how it goes before joining, but please Contact the club beforehand so we know you will be attending.

We can also provide further information about what to expect. Please take a look at our Running Schedule to see where we are meeting on a particular club night. LDR endeavour to make the club runs fun and varied, and we regularly regroup during the runs to make sure no-one is left behind.

Membership of LDR includes affiliation with England Athletics, members only running events; like our predictor run; lots of variety of structured and social runs, off-road and trail running, fell racing, club social events etc. The club are also involved with a number of local charities, such as our annual food bank run, and our club hosted Christmas Cross Country.

Club championship 2023

The 2023 LDR Club Championship is now online. There are 21 races currently on the calendar, which include the LWAC 5K and Lincs Cross Country series, where completing all the races in a series earns a bonus 5 points.

This year we also allocate points for the best ParkRun times on the year, and also for attendance at ParkRuns (either running or volunteering). There are also points for completing a marathon during the year.

As usual, the best 10 results count towards the final championship standings. Where there is an overlap with the club track nights (for example the LWAC 5K series) you will have the option to run the distance on ther track and the time will still count as normal. Well done to everyone who took park in any of the rounds of the LDR 2022 Championship. We had over 75 members take part, which is a great acheivement.
